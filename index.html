Puri Sabzi

Puri sabzi is a quintessential Indian dish that combines deep-fried bread (puri) with a flavorful vegetable curry (sabzi). The puris are made from unleavened dough, fried until golden and crispy, while the sabzi is a delicious medley of spices and vegetables.
